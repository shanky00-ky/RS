About Volleyball

Volleyball is a dynamic team sport with two teams of six players each, separated by a net. The aim is to score points by sending the ball into the opposing team’s court while ensuring it hits the ground within the opponent’s side or forces an error.

History of Volleyball

Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan. Initially called "Mintonette," it was designed to combine aspects of basketball, tennis, and handball.

Basic Rules

  • Teams: So many team can participate in the Tournament. basic rule is the 1 team can only have 6 players
  • Objective: Score by hitting the ball into the opponent’s court.
  • Rallies: Teams can use a maximum of three touches to return the ball.
